History Of Ayodhya

In traditional history, Ayodhya was the early capital of the kingdom of Kosala, though in Buddhist times
(6th to 5th century bce) Shravasti became the kingdom's chief city.

What happend 500 years ago in Ayodhya ?

In 1528 A.D. Babur came to Ayodhya and halted here for a week. He destroyed the ancient temple and on its site built a mosque, still known as Babur's mosque.

Ram Lalla idols inside Babri Mosque - 1949

Idol of Lord Ram surfaces inside Babri Masjid. One Gopal Singh Visharad filed a petition before a Faizabad court to worship the deity. Hashim Ansari, a resident of Ayodhya, apprroached the court saying the idols should be removed and it be allowed to remain a masjid. The government locked the place but priests were allowed to perform daily puja.

Rama Janmabhoomi

Rama Janma Bhoomi is a temple of Lord Sree Ram and is believed to be the place where Lord Ram was born. It is believed to be discovered by King Vikramaditya.
