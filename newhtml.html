॥ राम राम रामेति - श्रीविष्णुसहस्रनामस्तोत्रम्॥


The very sacred sloka of Lord Sri Rama: Sri Rama Rama Rameti Rame Rame Manorame... if chanted daily, is equal to chanting Sri Vishnu Sahasra Nama Stotram.

श्री राम राम रामेति रमे रामे मनोरमे ।
सहस्रनाम तत् तुल्यं रामनाम वरानने ॥

Transliteration
śrī rāma rāma rāmeti rame rāme manorame ।
sahasranāma tat tulyam rāmanāma varānane ॥

English translation
In chanting his name again and again, 'srirama rama rama,' I discover joy in Lord Rama who pleases
my heart and whose face is a blessing.
His name is equal to the one thousand names of Lord Visnu.

Hindi translation
"राम राम राम" (राम का नाम) का ध्यान करने से, मेरा मन राम की दिव्य चेतना में लीन हो जाता है, जो कि पारलौकिक है।

This is mentioned by Lord Shiva to Goddess Parvati, as mentioned below:
Goddess Parvati asked "O lord what is the short method to recite Vishnu Sahasranaam (if I am not able).
What is to be chanted for it?"
ishwara uvAcha:
sri Rama rama ramethi rame rame manorame । sahasranama thatthulyam rama nama varanane ॥
Ishwara said, "O beautiful one, Chant Rama Rama Rama which rejoices the mind.
It is equal to thousand names of Vishnu (Sri Vishnu Sahasranamam)."

Source – श्रीविष्णुसहस्रनामस्तोत्रम्
